🔹 Assignment A: E-Commerce Experience Testing



Site: https://automationexercise.com

(Note: The site may occasionally be unstable — treat this as a real-world test constraint.)



Your Task:



You're expected to validate the overall user experience from product discovery to checkout. Design your automation to cover the following functional expectations:



🧩 Scenario 1: “I want to buy something”

A user wants to browse categories and search for a product that matches their needs.
Once they find the product, they wish to:
View its details
Add it to the cart
Review their cart
The cart should reflect accurate quantities and totals.
🧩 **Scenario 2: “Am I logged in or not?”

Navigate through both authenticated and guest flows.
Try registering a new user — ensure your test handles both:
Fresh registration
Already-existing email addresses
🧩 Scenario 3: What breaks when things go wrong?

Try entering invalid values in a form (e.g., missing fields, malformed email).
Trigger at least one known client-side validation and verify error handling.
